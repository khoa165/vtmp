### Reflecting on Our Journey

Looking back, I am incredibly proud of how far we have come. When Huy Anhh and I first discussed the idea of offering completely free, long-term mentorship 2 years ago, many doubted whether we could pull it off. I vividly remember sharing our vision with a few industry professionals I admired, only to be met with skepticism. Some believed that, given our mentees' backgrounds, it would be unlikely to help even a third of them secure offers, and they doubted that more than one or two mentees would ever return to mentor future cohorts. However, that only further fuelled my determination and motivated me to pour my heart into this initiative. Fast forward to today, I am so proud that 100% of our successful mentees from the 2023 cohort returned as mentors and many of our current mentees are already committed to coming back in 2025 to help the next cohort.

Although the program is entirely free, I have always maintained a quality-first philosophy to ensure that mentees are well-prepared to succeed. Over the past two years, 38 mentees have joined us, collectively securing 83 internship and full-time offers in software engineering, many from some of the most prestigious tech companies. However, while these achievements are an important metric to share externally, I value the journey just as much, if not more, than the end results.

Having experienced the difficulties of navigating my early career without guidance and support, I care deeply about making this path easier for others. My focus lies in fostering an environment that emphasizes personal and professional growth, while building a strong, supportive and inclusive community. For me, success means seeing each mentee leave the program as a better engineer and a more confident individual, equipped not only with technical skills but also with the connections and camaraderie to thrive in their careers.
